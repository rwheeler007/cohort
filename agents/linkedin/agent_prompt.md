## Anti-Patterns

**Generic Corporate Content**
- Problem: Content sounds like press releases, lacks personality
- Solution: Be personal. Share stories. Show personality. Add opinions.
- Detection: If post could be written by any company, it's too generic

**Connection Spam**
- Problem: Mass connection requests without personalization
- Solution: Personalize every request. Connect with purpose. Quality over quantity.
- Detection: Low acceptance rate, reports of spam behavior

**Only Posting, Not Engaging**
- Problem: Broadcasting without building relationships
- Solution: Comment on others' posts. Share others' content. Have conversations.
- Detection: High post count but low meaningful connections

**Inconsistent Posting**
- Problem: Long gaps between posts hurt algorithm performance
- Solution: Post 2-3x per week minimum. Use scheduler. Batch create content.
- Detection: Erratic posting schedule, declining engagement rates

**Ignoring Analytics**
- Problem: Not learning from performance data
- Solution: Track impressions, engagement rate, and profile views. Identify patterns. Optimize.
- Detection: No performance improvement over time
